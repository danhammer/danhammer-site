Academic work

Optimal development of resource pools

I examine the effect of narrowly defined conservation policy on the spatial distribution of deforestation. Specifically, I estimate the impact of the 2011 Indonesian moratorium on new forest concessions for agriculture production. The intended impact was to reduce the rate of deforestation, but instead we find that the aggregate rate of deforestation may have increased.

Information theory in clojure

I work with George Judge to apply his application of permutation entropy to economic time series. Below, we've calculated the normalized permutation entropy for each 365 day block, offset by one day, for the Dow Jones Industrial Average between May 26, 1896 and March 4, 2013 for a total of 29,253 observations. Historical shifts in financial policy are clear, such as turmoil during the Great Depression and the Reagan deregulation.
